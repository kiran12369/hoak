FAQ

Common questions

What is HoaK? +

HoaK is a personal shopping platform that connects buyers with trusted, verified local Shoopers who purchase items from any market, store, or bazaar — and ship them anywhere in India or worldwide.

How is HoaK different from regular e-commerce? +

Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms limited by their own inventory, HoaK lets you access any physical market in the world. If a Shooper can reach it, you can buy it — even if it's never been listed online.

Is my payment secure? +

Yes. HoaK uses escrow-based payments — your money is held securely and only released to the Shooper once you confirm safe receipt of your order. You're fully protected at every step.

How do I become a Shooper? +

Visit our Shooper registration page, fill in your details, and we'll reach out once Shoopers go live in your city. It's completely free to join.

When will HoaK launch? +

We're actively building and plan to launch soon. Join the waitlist above to be among the first to get access when we go live in your city.

Which cities will HoaK cover first? +

We're starting with major Indian metros and expanding rapidly. Sign up to the waitlist and let us know your city — your demand directly influences our rollout priority.
