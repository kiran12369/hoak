How It Works

Three steps to get anything

No matter where an item is sold — local market, city bazaar, or another country — HoaK gets it to you.

1

Request an Item

Tell us what you want and where it's available. Our platform matches your request to a Shooper near the source.

2

Shooper Buys It

A verified local Shooper purchases the item on your behalf — from any store, market, or location you specify.

3

It Ships to You

The Shooper ships your item directly. Track it every step of the way, from purchase to your doorstep.

About HoaK

Redefining how the world shops locally

HoaK is a next-generation personal shopping platform designed to break down the barriers of geography in e-commerce. We believe every product — no matter where it's sold — should be accessible to any buyer anywhere in the world.

Built in India, designed for the world. HoaK bridges the gap between local markets and global consumers through a network of trusted Shoopers who make cross-border and cross-city shopping effortless.

Ravikiran
Founder & CEO

Driven by a vision to make every local market globally accessible, Ravikiran founded HoaK to empower both buyers who want unique products and everyday people who can earn by shopping locally for others.
