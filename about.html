Our Story

Redefining how the world shops locally

Built in India. Designed for the world. Powered by people.

Our Mission

Make every local market globally accessible

HoaK was born from a simple frustration: the most authentic, unique, and culturally rich products in the world are found in local markets — and they have no online presence.

The famous silk weavers in Varanasi don't have a website. The leather artisans in Dharavi don't sell on Amazon. The handblock printers of Bagru aren't on Instagram. But their products are extraordinary — and people all over the world want them.

HoaK bridges this gap. We're not just another delivery platform. We're building a new economy — one where proximity to a great market is an asset you can monetise, and where no product is truly out of reach.

Founder

The person behind HoaK

R
Ravikiran
Founder & CEO, HoaK

"I grew up watching my mother search for specific fabrics only available in one corner of one bazaar in a city three hours away. She knew exactly where they were, but getting there was the problem. That's what HoaK solves — not just for her, but for every person in the world who knows what they want and just needs someone local to get it for them."

"We're building HoaK to be a platform that creates genuine economic opportunity for local people while giving global buyers access to the world's most authentic products. That's a business worth building."

Our Journey

Building HoaK

Early 2025

The idea is born

Ravikiran identifies the gap between local bazaar access and global demand. Research begins into the trust and logistics infrastructure required.

Mid 2025

Platform architecture & design

Core product design finalized. Escrow payment model developed. Shooper verification framework built.

Late 2025

Waitlist launched

2,400+ people join the waitlist. 12 cities see Shooper applications. Feedback shapes the product roadmap.

2026

Public launch — coming soon

Starting with Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, and Jaipur. Then expanding rapidly across India and internationally.
