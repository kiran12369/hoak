The Process

How HoaK Works

From request to doorstep — a simple, safe, transparent process in three steps.

01

Request an Item

Tell HoaK what you want and where it's available. Be as specific as you like — product name, store, market, city, or even a photo. Our platform instantly matches your request to a verified Shooper in that location.

You'll receive a quote covering the item price, Shooper commission, and estimated shipping — all before you confirm. No surprises, ever.

📝
Describe your itemProduct name, store, market, or photo
📍
Specify the locationCity, bazaar, specific shop — as precise as you like
💰
Get a full quoteItem price + Shooper fee + shipping — all upfront
✅
Confirm & pay securelyFunds held in escrow until you receive your order
02

Shooper Buys It

A verified local Shooper — background-checked, rated, and reviewed — physically goes to the market or store and purchases your item. You receive real-time photo confirmation the moment they buy it.

You can chat with your Shooper directly through the app to clarify details, ask for photos of specific items, or check alternatives if your first choice isn't available.

🤝
Matched instantlyOur algorithm finds the best Shooper for your request
🛒
Physical purchaseShooper goes in person — any store, market, or stall
📸
Photo confirmationReal-time photos sent before funds are released
💬
Direct chatMessage your Shooper any time through the app
03

It Ships to You

Your item is professionally packaged and handed to our logistics partner. Full tracking is available from the moment it leaves the Shooper's hands to the moment it arrives at your door.

For international deliveries, we provide full customs documentation guidance. Escrow funds are only released to the Shooper once you confirm receipt — you're always protected.
